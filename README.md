5. Dashboard Features & Visuals

Sales by City (Map Chart)
Displays geographic distribution of sales across cities.

Profit by Region (Clustered Bar Chart)
Compares profit performance across regions (East, West, North, South).

Sales vs Discount vs Profit (Scatter Chart)
Shows relationship between discount given and resulting sales & profit.

Profit by Sub-Category (Horizontal Bar Chart)
Identifies most profitable product sub-categories.

Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
Compares sales across Office Supplies, Furniture, and Technology.

Sales by Segment (Donut Chart)
Shows contribution from Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments.

Interactive Slicers
